# About Ameano Peptides

## Who We Are

At **AmeanoPeptides.com**, we supply **high-purity, research-only peptides** backed by science, rigorous quality controls, and transparent documentation. Whether you’re an independent researcher, a university lab, or a commercial R&D team, our aim is simple: deliver consistently pure peptides that advance your work.

### What We Stand For

- **Scientific Rigor** – Every batch is ≥99 % pure and shipped with a full Certificate of Analysis (COA).
- **Integrity & Transparency** – Open disclosure of sourcing, testing methodology, and quality data.
- **Reliable Fulfillment** – Same-day dispatch on qualifying orders keeps time-sensitive projects on track.
- **Research-Focused Support** – A knowledgeable team ready to help with product selection and documentation.

### Our Manufacturing Process

1. **Laboratory-Grade Purified Water** – Provides a contaminant-free foundation.
2. **Stabilizing Excipients** – Precisely measured mannitol and sucrose protect peptide integrity during lyophilization.
3. **Analytical Verification** – Electrospray ionization mass spectrometry (ESI-MS) confirms molecular structure.
4. **0.22 µm Filtration** – Removes particulates prior to vialing.
5. **Lyophilization & Vialing** – A controlled 36-hour freeze-dry cycle maximizes long-term stability.

> _“At Ameano Peptides, advancing research is not just our business—it’s our purpose.”_

Our mission is to accelerate scientific discovery by providing consistent, well-documented peptides and responsive customer care. Full control over production, combined with transparent data, gives researchers the confidence they need.
